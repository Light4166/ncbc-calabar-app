Who We Are

Guided by God’s Word to make a lasting impact in Calabar and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant Christ-centered community devoted to spreading the Gospel, fostering genuine spiritual growth, and transforming lives through the love, grace, and truth of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To glorify God by reaching the unreached, discipling believers into spiritual maturity, and equipping every member for impactful service within our church, local community, and the world.
